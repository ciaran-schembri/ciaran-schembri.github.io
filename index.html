Math Research

I am a Research Scientist at Dartmouth College, working with John Voight as part of the Simons Collaboration on arithmetic geometry, number theory and computation. My research interests centre around the arithmetic of abelian varieties, Galois representations and moduli spaces (especially modular and Shimura curves).
